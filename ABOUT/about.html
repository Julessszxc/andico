About.

An aspiring Game Developer

I am Christian Jules G. Andico

I am 19 years old, born on November 21, 2001. I live in Zone 4 Calong-calong, Barangay Singcang Airport, Bacolod City. I am currently living with my family. My mother is Shiela G. Andico and my father is Jose Maria Joy L. Andico. I have 2 siblings, they are Christopher Lloyd, who is in high school and Aliyah Shane who is in elementary. I am the oldest among my siblings. My father went abroad when I was still in kindergarten, in order for him to support us.

I graduated elementary in Education and Training Center School III. Back then, it was an innocent me that does not want to go to school and instead just wants to play and have fun. I then went to Negros Occidental High school for both my junior high school and senior high school. It was one of my most memorable experiences. I did really grow there and I did many mistakes but I learned from them. It was a turning point in my life. I experience my first heart break, getting honors in the end of the school year, finding true friends and the things that I went through, it made me stronger.

My Current Journey

My goal in life is to be a professional programmer in the area of game development. My plans in life are, to finish college, to do my dream job and to marry and have my own family and also someday, I can pay back the hard work and sacrifices my family has done to me.
